Privacy Policy for Paws Vs Claws

Effective date: 02/14/2026

Dumhawk (“we,” “us”) operates the mobile application Paws Vs Claws (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information Collected

We do not require an account and we do not directly collect personal information like your name, email, or address.

The App shows advertisements using Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads). AdMob may automatically collect certain information to provide and measure ads, including:

  • Device information (such as device type, OS version)
  • IP address (which may be used to estimate general location)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA, if you allow tracking on iOS)
  • Information about ad interactions (e.g., which ads were shown, taps, and performance)
  • Diagnostic data to help prevent fraud and ensure ad security

How Information Is Used

Information collected by AdMob may be used to:

  • Display ads in the App
  • Measure ad performance and improve ad relevance
  • Limit how often you see the same ad (frequency capping)
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or security issues

Tracking and Your Choices (iOS)

If the App requests permission to track you (App Tracking Transparency), you can choose Allow or Ask App Not to Track. You can change this anytime in iOS Settings.

Depending on your settings and region, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads) for advertising

AdMob’s data practices are governed by Google’s own policies.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us at dumhawk@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above when changes are made.
