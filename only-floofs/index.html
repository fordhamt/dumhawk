FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is Only Floofs?

Only Floofs is a free iOS app for discovering the internet's cutest pets. You scroll an endless feed of cats and dogs, heart your favorites, follow the pets you love, and can post your own pet to climb the leaderboards.

What is a floof?

Floof is internet slang for any adorably cute animal. Cats, dogs, bunnies, birds, reptiles, if it is cute, it is a floof. And every one of them belongs on Only Floofs.

Is Only Floofs free?

Yes. Browsing is completely free and needs no account. You sign in only if you want to post a pet or follow your favorites.

What animals are on Only Floofs?

Mostly cats and dogs, but it's not only paws. The community also shares reptiles, birds, bunnies, and other animals. Every photo passes automatic safety checks before it goes live.

How do I make my pet famous?

Download the app, post a photo of your pet, and share it. Likes and hearts from people around the world push your pet up the daily, weekly, and all-time leaderboards, and one lucky floof is featured every day.

Do I need an account?

No account is needed to browse, swipe, and heart. An account is required only to post pets or keep a profile.
