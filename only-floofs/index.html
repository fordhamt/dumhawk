What is Only Floofs?

Only Floofs is a never-ending feed of adorable pets. Swipe through real pets, double-tap to shower the ones you love with hearts, follow your favorite floofs, and climb the daily, weekly, and all-time leaderboards, including the charts in your own city.

  • Scroll the cutest feed around. A full-screen stream of real pets that never runs out.
  • Give them all the hearts. Every like and heart lifts your favorites higher.
  • Make your pet famous. Post in seconds and rack up fans from around the world.
  • Follow pets, not people. The pets are the stars here.
  • Safe by design. Every photo clears automatic safety checks before it's ever public.
  • No account needed to look. Browse, swipe, and heart freely. Sign in only to post or follow.
